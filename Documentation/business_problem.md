
# Business Problem

Real estate developers often manage inventory, bookings, and payments using spreadsheets.
This approach causes:

- Data inconsistency
- Lack of centralized tracking
- Manual payment allocation
- Poor visibility of revenue and inventory
- Limited analytics for decision making

## Objective

Build a centralized data platform that:

- Tracks unit inventory across projects and blocks
- Manages booking lifecycle
- Tracks customer payments and schedules
- Automates agreement eligibility
- Generates financial and operational KPIs
- Supports BI dashboards for management insights

## Expected Impact

- Single source of truth for real estate operations
- Automated workflows for bookings and payments
- Real-time analytics dashboards
- Improved financial visibility
